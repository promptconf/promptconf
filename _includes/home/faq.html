Do I have to be a coder to submit a talk?
No, you don't have to be a coder to submit! We do want the talk to be related to programming, web development, web design, computing, etc. but no you do not have to be a coder yourself.
How long will the talks be?
All talks will be 10 minutes in length! This is with the exception of the keynote presentation(s), which will be 25 minutes each.
